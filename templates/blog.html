The Power of AI in Healthcare

With advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning, diagnosing diseases has become more accurate and accessible. Our symptom-based disease diagnosis system uses a Decision Tree Classifier model to predict possible diseases based on user input.

Key Features of Our System

  • User-friendly interface for symptom input
  • AI-powered predictions using a trained model
  • Provides disease information and preventive measures

How It Works

  1. Users enter their symptoms into the web application.
  2. The system processes the input and maps symptoms to known diseases.
  3. The AI model predicts the possible disease and provides additional information.

Future Improvements

We plan to enhance the accuracy by integrating more advanced models like Neural Networks and expanding our dataset for better disease prediction.
